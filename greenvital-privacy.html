This policy applies to the GreenVital natural remedies app by GreenVital LLC.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how GreenVital ("we", "us", "our"), a product of GreenVital LLC, handles information when you use the GreenVital mobile app.

Based on the current Google Play Store data safety declaration for this app, GreenVital does not collect personal data from users through the app and does not share user data with third parties.

2. Data Collection and Sharing

In the current app configuration, GreenVital is designed to work primarily as an offline educational resource.

  • No in-app account registration is required to browse remedies.
  • No personal data is collected by GreenVital through normal app use.
  • No user data is shared with third parties by GreenVital.

If this changes in a future version, we will update this Privacy Policy and the app’s store listing accordingly.

3. Local Features and Device Storage

GreenVital may use local device storage to support app features such as saved favorites, preferences, selected language, or reminder settings.

Local information used for these features stays on your device unless a future version of the app clearly states otherwise.

4. Notifications and Reminders

If you enable reminders, the app may create local notifications on your device to help you remember wellness routines or saved items. These reminders are part of the on-device app experience.

5. Purchases and Platform Providers

GreenVital may offer premium content or features. Any in-app purchases are processed by the platform provider, such as Google Play, and not directly by GreenVital.

Google may process billing-related information according to its own policies and terms. Please review the applicable platform policies for those services.

6. Children and Sensitive Health Information

GreenVital is an educational wellness resource and is not intended to collect medical records, diagnosis information, or emergency health data.

7. Your Choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can remove the app from your device to delete local app data.
  • You can manage notification permissions through your device settings.
  • You can manage premium purchases through your Google Play account.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date on this page.
