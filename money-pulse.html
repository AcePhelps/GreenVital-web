Overview

A stronger view of where the month stands.

Money Pulse front-loads the information that matters most. Users can see whether the month is trending up or down, then move naturally into income, spending, bank status, and transaction history.

  • Centers the monthly result instead of hiding it in reports.
  • Connects balance, spending, and activity in one flow.
  • Uses a darker, more focused visual system for stronger contrast.

About

A finance product that feels immediate.

Money Pulse takes on a more concentrated tone inside the GreenVital family. The goal is to make money feel more readable and less intimidating.
