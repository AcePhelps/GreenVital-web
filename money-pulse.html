Overview

Money Pulse is built around instant readability. The app centers the month’s result first, then supports that with income, spending, bank connection status, and recent transactions.

  • Front-loads the monthly trend instead of hiding it in reports.
  • Connects transaction history with higher-level context.
  • Uses a dark, focused visual language to reduce noise.

About

Money Pulse is part of the GreenVital family, but it takes on a more concentrated and data-first tone. It is intended to make money feel more legible, not more intimidating.

Product intent

The design emphasizes emotional clarity: users should know where they stand before they start exploring details.
