MoneyPulse - FAQ & Support

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Answers to common questions about MoneyPulse and how to contact support.

Support email: support@greenvital.app

Typical response time: 1-3 business days

We can help with: account access, subscriptions, billing questions, bank connection issues, data deletion requests, and bug reports.

Getting Started

What is MoneyPulse?

MoneyPulse is a personal finance app that helps you connect financial accounts, review transactions, identify recurring subscriptions, and monitor monthly income, spending, and net cash flow.

Which banks can I connect?

MoneyPulse uses Plaid to connect to thousands of supported banks and credit unions. Availability depends on each institution and region.

Do I need to connect a bank?

You can browse parts of MoneyPulse without connecting a bank, but core financial insights depend on connected account data.

Security & Privacy

Is it safe to connect my bank?

Bank linking is handled by Plaid. MoneyPulse does not collect or store your bank login credentials.

Can MoneyPulse move money or make payments?

No. MoneyPulse is read-only. It does not initiate transfers or payments.

Does MoneyPulse use biometrics?

If enabled in app settings and supported by your device, MoneyPulse can require biometric authentication to open sensitive flows.

Transactions, Spending & Insights

What information does MoneyPulse show?

MoneyPulse focuses on transactions, recurring subscriptions, monthly spending, inflows, outflows, and monthly net cash flow based on connected account activity.

Does MoneyPulse show my account balances?

MoneyPulse may use connected account data to support app features, but the core experience is focused on transaction history, recurring charges, and monthly financial insights rather than full banking functionality.

How is monthly net calculated?

Monthly net equals inflows such as income, deposits, and refunds minus outflows such as spending and withdrawals for the selected month.

Why is a transaction missing or delayed?

Banks may delay posting. You can use Sync now in Settings to request a refresh. Some transactions may appear as pending first.

How are transfers treated?

Money moved between your own connected accounts may be excluded from spending and income calculations when both sides are available, to reduce double-counting.

Subscriptions & Billing

Does MoneyPulse offer a subscription?

Yes. Some features may require an active subscription.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple App Store or Google Play account settings, depending on where you purchased the subscription.

Can MoneyPulse issue refunds?

Refunds are handled by the platform provider according to its billing rules. Please refer to Apple or Google Play support for billing disputes.

Account, Data & Support

How do I delete my account?

Use the in-app account deletion option if available. If you cannot access the app, email support@greenvital.app with the subject Account Deletion Request.

How do I contact support?

Email: support@greenvital.app
We can help with account access, subscriptions, billing questions, bank-linking issues, data deletion requests, and bug reports.
Please include issue details and screenshots if possible, but do not include bank passwords, full account numbers, or other sensitive credentials.

What should I include in a support request?

Include your device type, app version if known, a short description of the problem, and any screenshots that help explain the issue.

If you still need help, contact support@greenvital.app.
