Overview

Designed to feel calm the moment it opens.

The product centers immediate clarity. Users can see whether protection is active, review boundaries for the day, and make small edits without breaking their flow.

  • Shows current protection state at a glance.
  • Highlights routines like morning, work, and sleep boundaries.
  • Keeps the interface soft, quiet, and highly legible.

About

A supportive product, not a noisy one.

Back to Life is part of GreenVital’s effort to make digital tools feel more grounding and less demanding. The interface is intentionally bright, open, and easy to trust at a glance.
