Overview

Back to Life gives users a calm way to understand their current protection status, review configured boundaries, and make small changes without cognitive overload.

  • Shows protection state immediately on the main screen.
  • Highlights boundary presets like morning, work, and sleep.
  • Keeps editing fast so routine changes stay lightweight.

About

The app is part of GreenVital’s effort to make digital tools feel more supportive and less draining. The interface is intentionally soft, bright, and legible so it feels trustworthy at a glance.

Product intent

Back to Life is built around a simple idea: protective structure should feel like relief, not complexity.
